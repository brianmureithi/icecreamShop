Our History

Sweet Life Ice cream shop is a dream culiminating a journey that started eight years ago. The shop began as a small truck and has slowly grown to become a hub for the best ice cream in town. We deal in a variety of ice cream like vanilla, chocolate, blueberry, among other flavours as will be shown below. Thank you for taking the time to view our catalogue, enjoy

Available flavours

Chocolote Flavour

Chocolate icecream is one of the most common flavours after vanilla. It is made using a blend of cocoa powder along with eggs, cream, vanilla, and sugar. Other flavours of chocholate such as mint chocolate are also available.

Vanilla Flavour

Vanilla ice cream is the most common flavour of ice cream which is favourite amongst all. Vanilla ice cream is made by blending in vanilla essence with the eggs, cream, milk and sugar. The vanilla essence gives ice cream a natural aroma and vanilla flavour.

Blue Berry Flavour

Blueberry ice cream is made out of the blueberry fruit. It can be made at home with fresh or frozen blueberries. The ice cream has a gorgeous, purplish-blue color and may have a topping made of mixed fresh berries or blueberry sauce.

Chocolote sticks

Chocolate icecream is one of the most common flavours after vanilla. It is made using a blend of cocoa powder along with eggs, cream, vanilla, and sugar. Other flavours of chocholate such as mint chocolate are also available.

Strawberry

Strawberry ice cream is a flavor of ice cream made with strawberry or strawberry flavoring. It is made by blending in fresh strawberries or strawberry flavoring with the eggs, cream, vanilla and sugar used to make ice cream

Vanilla Caramel

A combination of vanilla and caramel flavours. It is made using a blend of cocoa powder along with caramel, eggs, cream, vanilla, and sugar. Other combination of flavours such as this are also available. The caramel is salted and has butter
